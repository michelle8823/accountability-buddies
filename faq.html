    How much does it cost to create an account?

    Nothing! Everything is free of cost.

    How do I delete my account?

    Go to Users → Settings → Delete account permenantly
    Once you delete your account, there is no way to recover it.

    How do I change my preference or goals?

    Go to Users → Prefernce and edit your details.
    Once you update your preference or goals, your current buddy match will be cancelled and we will find a new list of buddies to be matched with you.

    When can I expect to get a list of matching buddies?

    It can take up to a week to match you with other buddies, depending on supply and demand. If you have not receive any email from us after a week, please send an email to help@accbud.com explaining your situation.

    I've chosen my buddy but they haven't respond my request.

    Buddy requests that are not responded within 5 days will be automatically cancelled. You will then be sent another email to be matched with other buddies.

    Can I choose more than one buddy?

    Yes you can. However, you can only have one buddy per goal for now. Whoever accepts your request first will be your buddy.

    What information will be provided to my buddy?

    Only your name and email address will be provided. We expect you to first contact each other using email.
